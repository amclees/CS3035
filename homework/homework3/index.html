Text Adventure

This version of text adventure is based in JQuery. You may use the WASD keys, arrow keys, or the buttons below to move. The combat is a simplified version of D&D combat. A 20 sided dice is rolled by the attacker, the attacker's attack bonus is added, and, if it is higher than the defender's AC, the defender takes a six sided dice plus the attacker's damage bonus worth of damage. Your inventory is displayed below the map. Your goal is to kill the final boss, Oboro.
